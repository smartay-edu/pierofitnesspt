About Me

My name is Piero, I've been in the fitness industry for over seven years. I come for a background of playing competitive football to a very high level. Since having to hang up my football boots I found my passion in the gym and helping others achieve their fitness goals


I've worked with a variety of face to face and online clients with various different goals such as:


  • > Weight loss to get lean
  • > Weight gain to gain muscle
  • > Body transformations clients


Now I've taken the step to help clients online to try and reach more people and change more lives and achieve more goals.
